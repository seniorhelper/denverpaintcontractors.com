Our Story

Three Decades of Colorado Craftsmanship

Our owner didn't stumble into the painting trade. He grew up in Colorado, learned the contracting business from the ground up, and spent over 30 years sharpening every skill that goes into a truly exceptional paint job. As Denver painters, we've worked on everything from modest starter homes in Lakewood to sprawling custom properties in the foothills — and we approach every single project with the same standard.

That standard is simple: your home deserves the same quality work he would do on his own. Consequently, Denver Paint Contractors has never chased volume at the expense of quality. We take on the jobs we can do right — and we do them right, every time.

"I've spent 30 years in this trade because I genuinely love the transformation. A fresh coat of paint doesn't just change how a home looks — it changes how a family feels about where they live."

Denver Paint Contractors is a family owned and operated business. That means when you call us, you're not reaching a call center or a national franchise. You're talking to us — the people who will actually show up to your home, manage your project, and stand behind the finished result. Furthermore, it means that our reputation in this community is deeply personal. We live here. We raise our families here. Your experience with us reflects directly on who we are as people — not just as a company.

Additionally, growing up in Colorado gives us a real edge when it comes to the unique demands of painting in this climate. We understand how Denver's intense UV radiation, dramatic temperature swings, and freeze-thaw cycles affect paint adhesion and longevity. Therefore, we select products and preparation methods specifically suited to Colorado's conditions — not just what works in a more forgiving climate.
