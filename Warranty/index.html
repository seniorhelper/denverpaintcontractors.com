What's Covered vs. What's Not

Full Transparency on Every Term

We believe a warranty is only as good as its clarity. Therefore, we spell out exactly what's covered and what isn't — no fine print surprises, no runaround when you need us.

✅
Covered by Warranty
  • ✓Premature peeling or flaking of paint caused by our application
  • ✓Blistering or bubbling due to faulty surface preparation
  • ✓Cracking or checking caused by improper paint application technique
  • ✓Paint adhesion failures on surfaces we prepared and painted
  • ✓Runs, sags, or drips missed during our quality inspection
  • ✓Improper coverage — holidays, thin spots, visible bare areas
  • ✓Lap marks or brush stroke visibility caused by technique errors
  • ✓Bleed-through of stains we were responsible for sealing
  • ✓Caulking failures at areas we sealed during the project
  • ✓Any defect in workmanship identified within five years of completion
❌
Not Covered by Warranty
  • ✗Normal wear and tear over time, including gradual color fading
  • ✗UV fading or chalking caused by prolonged sun exposure
  • ✗Damage caused by hail, storms, flooding, fire, or other acts of nature
  • ✗Physical damage from impact, vandalism, or accidents after completion
  • ✗Mold, mildew, or algae growth on painted surfaces after project completion
  • ✗Damage caused by paint or materials supplied by the homeowner
  • ✗Modifications to painted surfaces by the homeowner or third parties
  • ✗Pre-existing structural issues such as wood rot, moisture intrusion, or substrate failure
  • ✗Issues arising from conditions disclosed and accepted in writing before work began
  • ✗Paint manufacturer product defects (covered separately by manufacturer warranty)

Warranty FAQ

Your Warranty Questions — Answered

Everything you need to know about your 5-year workmanship warranty, clearly explained.

What exactly does the 5-year workmanship warranty cover?+
The warranty covers defects directly caused by our painting workmanship — including premature peeling, blistering, bubbling, cracking, flaking, and paint adhesion failures. If any of these issues appear within five years of project completion and are determined to be caused by our workmanship, we return and repair it at absolutely no charge to you. This applies to all services we perform.
How fast will you respond to my warranty claim?+
We guarantee a maximum 7 calendar day (1 week) response time on all warranty claims. After you contact us, we confirm receipt, schedule an on-site assessment, and get to your property within that window. Furthermore, in most cases we respond within 24 to 48 hours — the 7-day commitment is our maximum guarantee, not our typical turnaround.
What is NOT covered by the workmanship warranty?+
The warranty does not cover natural wear and tear, gradual UV fading or chalking from prolonged sun exposure, damage caused by hail, storms, flooding, fire, or other acts of nature, physical damage from impact or accidents after completion, mold or mildew growth after the project, modifications made by others to the painted surface, pre-existing structural issues such as wood rot or moisture intrusion, or defects in paint products themselves (which are covered separately by the paint manufacturer's warranty).
Is the warranty transferable if I sell my home?+
Yes — our 5-year workmanship warranty is fully transferable to a new homeowner if the property is sold during the warranty period. Simply contact us with the new owner's name and contact information and we will update our records at no charge. This transferability is a genuine selling advantage when listing your home.
How do I file a warranty claim?+
Filing a claim is simple. Call us at 720-208-5645, email quote@painterhotline.com, or fill out the warranty claim form on this page. Include your name, property address, approximate project date, and a description of the issue you're seeing. We'll confirm receipt and schedule your assessment within 7 days — typically much sooner.
Does the warranty apply to exterior and interior painting?+
Yes. The 5-year workmanship warranty applies equally to all services we perform — interior painting, exterior painting, deck staining, door painting, cabinet painting, and commercial projects. There are no excluded service categories. Every project we complete is covered in full.
What paint brands do you use and do they have their own warranties?+
We use only premium paints from Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, Behr Premium Plus, and Valspar — all of which carry their own manufacturer product warranties separate from our workmanship warranty. Therefore, your project is doubly protected: our workmanship warranty covers application defects, and the paint manufacturer's warranty covers product failures. Together, this gives you comprehensive long-term protection.
Do I need my original invoice to file a warranty claim?+
It's helpful but not required. We maintain our own records of all completed projects. If you contact us with your name and property address, we can typically locate your project in our records. However, if you have your original invoice or project documentation handy, sharing it speeds up the verification process.
