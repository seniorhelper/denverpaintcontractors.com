The Backyard Deck

Denver Deck Staining & Exterior Specialists

Colorado's climate is exceptionally harsh on exterior wood. Consequently, your deck, fence, and exterior trim need professional care from contractors who understand what UV radiation at altitude does to wood fiber — and how to stop it.

  • Pressure washing and thorough cleaning before any stain application
  • Sanding and wood brightening to open grain for maximum penetration
  • Premium oil-based and water-based stains for Colorado conditions
  • Cedar, pine, pressure-treated, and composite deck expertise
  • Fence staining and refinishing throughout the Denver metro
  • Deck board replacement and minor repairs before staining
  • UV-blocking formulas rated for high-altitude Colorado exposure

A properly stained deck not only looks spectacular — it also adds to your home's value and lifespan. Furthermore, proper staining prevents costly structural rot that can turn a $600 maintenance project into a $6,000 deck replacement. Prevention is always far more affordable than repair.

How much does it cost to paint a house in Denver? +
Interior house painting in Denver typically ranges from $1.50 to $3.50 per square foot, depending on the number of rooms, ceiling height, and prep work needed. Exterior painting generally ranges from $1.80 to $4.00 per square foot. However, every home is different. Therefore, the most accurate way to get a number is to call us at 720-208-5645 or submit our online form for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific project.
Are you licensed and insured to paint in Denver? +
Yes, absolutely. We are fully licensed and insured painting contractors in Denver, Colorado. Every project is covered by comprehensive liability insurance, and our painters are experienced professionals — not subcontractors hired off the street. Your home and investment are fully protected. Furthermore, we always carry proof of insurance and will provide it to you before any work begins.
What is your 5-Year Workmanship Warranty? +
Our 5-Year Workmanship Warranty covers defects arising from our workmanship — including peeling, blistering, and adhesion failures. If any covered issue appears within five years of project completion, we return and fix it at absolutely no charge. This warranty is separate from the manufacturer's warranty on paint products themselves, which additionally covers paint failures. Visit our Warranty page for the full details.
What paint brands do Denver Paint Contractors use? +
We use only premium paints from industry-leading brands: Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, Behr Premium Plus, and Valspar. We never substitute cheap discount paints to pad our margins. The quality of the paint directly affects how good the project looks and how long it lasts — therefore, using premium materials isn't optional for us. It's a non-negotiable standard.
Do you offer deck staining and wood refinishing? +
Yes — deck staining and refinishing is one of our specialty services. We pressure wash, clean, sand, and apply premium deck stains specifically formulated to withstand Colorado's intense UV rays, dramatic temperature swings, and freeze-thaw cycles. We work with cedar, pine, pressure-treated lumber, and composite decking. Additionally, we handle fence staining throughout the Denver metro.
How long does an exterior paint job take? +
Most exterior residential paint jobs in Denver take between 2 and 5 days, depending on the size of your home, number of stories, surface preparation required, and current weather conditions. We work efficiently without ever sacrificing workmanship. Moreover, Colorado weather can be unpredictable — therefore, we monitor forecasts closely and always account for proper drying conditions between coats.
